OUR SERVICES

ACUPUNCTURE THERAPY

Acupuncture is a form of alternative medicine and a component of traditional Chinese medicine in which thin needles are inserted into the body

CUPPING THERAPY

Cupping therapy is a form of alternative medicine in which a local suction is created on the skin with the application of heated cups

AROMATHERAPY

Aromatherapy is based on the usage of aromatic materials including essential oils and other aroma compounds

PHYTOTHERAPY

the use of plants or herbs to manage health conditions. It also refers to substances that come from plants or herbs
